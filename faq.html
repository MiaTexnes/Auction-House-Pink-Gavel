Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Pink Gavel Auctions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

To place a bid, simply navigate to the item's page and enter your bid amount in the bidding section. Make sure your bid meets the minimum requirement and that you have sufficient credits in your account. Click "Place Bid" to confirm your bid.

If someone places a higher bid than yours, you'll be notified and your credits will be returned to your account. You can then choose to place a higher bid if you wish to continue bidding on the item.

Once placed, bids are binding and cannot be canceled. This ensures fairness for all participants. Please make sure you're comfortable with your bid amount before confirming it.

Click the "Register" button in the top navigation or go to our registration page. Fill in your name, email, and choose a secure password. You'll receive 1000 free credits to start bidding!

Go to your profile page and click the "Edit Profile" button. You can update your avatar, bio, and other personal information. Changes are saved automatically.

From your profile page, click "New Listing" and fill in the item details including title, description, end date, and image URL. Make sure to provide clear, accurate information to attract bidders.

When your auction ends, the highest bidder wins the item and the credits are transferred to your account. You'll need to coordinate with the winner for item delivery outside of our platform.

Pink Gavel Auctions uses a credit-based system. You start with 1000 free credits upon registration. Credits are used to place bids and are transferred between users when auctions are won. Currently, additional credits cannot be purchased.

If you run out of credits, you can earn more by selling items through our auction platform. When your items are sold, you'll receive credits from the winning bidders. You can also wait for future features that may allow credit purchases.
